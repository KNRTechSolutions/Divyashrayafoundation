ABOUT US

Who we are?

We envision to grow as Women across the region, world eradicating major problem called poverty by giving a platform to the unrecognized and underprivileged women and addressing very basic but neglected issues faced by these people by introducing very unique and Empowering tools by involving them and making them independent financially.

What are our initiatives?

BPO centre - we run a BPO centre, where we help companies with lead generation, customer support by employing women. According to our survey there are many women who are very skilled, enthusiastic and dreams to work with companies but they cannot leave their home towns to go and work in metro cities, so we are giving them a right platform, where they can work from their places itself and support their families and fulfil their dreams.
Skill Development Center - there are many women from all the ages, who wants to pursue their dreams in jobs, business, but lack behind few skills and opportunities where they can accomplish their goals, so we at Divyashraya Foundation will identify such women, train them with required skills and hell them to accomplish their goals in business and jobs.

Food @5rs currently at Kalaburagi-

There are many people who are still not getting food, they are many people who cannot afford even one time meal and here comes these people who travel daily around 100 kms to work early in the morning just to earn few rupees and they don't even get time to cook and carry food to work places as their whole day goes in travelling and working and to have complete meal they have to spend around 50rs which they cannot afford everyday, so we have come up with a solution to provide them a healthy meal @ 5rs which they can afford.
For now our team is travelling in and around Kalaburagi to identify people who are actually in need of it. We will also keep providing free food to people who cannot even meet 5rs to get the food.



Divya Kulkarni

Founder Of Divyashraya Foundation

Divya Kulkarni, always believed in Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam - The World Is A Family. She sees everyone equally and empathise people's problems.
Seeing every woman around her, interacting with them, she identified, everyone has their goals(small or big) and they want to achieve them and become independent,
women from cities get the opportunities, but women from rural/semi urban regions, because of lack of proper platform, guidance and support they just sacrifice everything and lead a life, even though they have good skills and very talented. She then started interacting with many women and founded this Organization to support each women who need support, guidance and help. As the tagline says, by this foundation, we even provide food, grocery, clothes and other basic items to the people who are actually in need of it.
